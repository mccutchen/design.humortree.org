The Humor Tree Design Coalition

Here lie the mouldering remains of the Humor Tree Design Coalition, the best freelance web development firm that never was.

If we had spent less time working on a never-ending series of redesigns of our own web presence and more time finding and working for clients, maybe we would have become something great.

But that's not what we did, so here we present an archive of our many incarnations but none of our client work, most of which succumbed to bit rot many years ago. Versions are listed in reverse chronological order. All dates are approximate.

This is mostly a snapshot of Will McCutchen's design sensibilities and burgeoning JavaScript skills. Please note that the sensibilities and programming skills in question led mostly to ever-changing colors and patterns. And gray. Lots of gray.
