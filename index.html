The Humor Tree
Design Coalition

Here lie the moldering remains of the Humor Tree Design Coalition, the best freelance web development firm that never was.

If we had spent less time working on a never-ending series of redesigns of our own web presence and more time finding and working for clients, maybe we would have become something great.

But that's not what we did. Here we present what remains of our client work (most of which succumbed to bit-rot many years ago) and an archive of the many incarnations of our web site. Work is listed in reverse chronological order. All dates are approximate (or guesses).

Looking back over this collection of work, two themes seem stand out to me:

  1. Simplicity, especially in the client work. This is probably due in equal parts to our good(?) taste and our lack of real design skill and vision.
  2. Ever-changing colors and patterns, primarily in our work for ourselves. We’re not sure if this suggests an unfortunate inability to make decisions or an admirable longing for infinite possibility. Or ADHD.
  3. Gray. Lots of gray.
  4. Hey! I thought you said two themes!
