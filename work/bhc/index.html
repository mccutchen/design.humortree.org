From the President

Welcome to the Brookhaven College website. Regardless of your reason for visiting this electronic environment—personal college explorations, research about Brookhaven College programs and costs, information about our location and class schedules, even information about our faculty—we are glad you are investigating Brookhaven College.

Brookhaven College is a comprehensive community college offering good, solid transfer educational programs; vocational-technical programs in areas as broad as automotive technology, visual communications, and fashion merchandising; many levels of English as a second language instruction; and a varied array of non-credit options to teach you computer skills, prepare you for various national licensure exams, and help you prepare for a new career.

Brookhaven College is one of the seven colleges of the Dallas County Community College District. Educational and employment opportunities are offered by Brookhaven College without regard to race, color, age, national origin, religion, sex, disability or sexual orientation.
