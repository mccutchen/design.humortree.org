Useful Information
National Hospice Foundation
"The National Hospice Foundation (NHF), a 501 (C) (3) charitable organization, was created in 1992 to broaden America's understanding of hospice through research and education. Its mission is to expand America's vision for end of life care."

Texas Medicaid Program
"Medicaid is the State and Federal cooperative venture that provides medical coverage to eligible needy persons. Its purpose is to improve the health of people who might otherwise go without medical care for themselves and their children."

Bereavement.org
"An electronic book created to help those who grieve and those who provide support for the bereaved. It's purpose is to foster an understanding of the human response to the phenomena of death, dying and bereavement."

Medicare.org
A wealth of information on Medicare, including Medicare Education and Medicare News. The site includes a discussion board and Medicare Classifieds, as well as links to other Medicare resources.

The American Cancer Society
The homepage for the American Cancer Society. It has resources for professionals and volunteers, information on cancer and cancer treatment, and the latest news in the pertaining to cancer.
