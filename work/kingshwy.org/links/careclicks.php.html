CareClicks and Kings Highway

About CareClicks.com™ LLC

CareClicks.com™ LLC is a for-profit e-commerce marketing company. Our corporate goal is to assist nonprofit organizations in achieving their vision and goals, without asking loyal supporters to expend additional financial resources. Utilizing the power of group purchasing, we facilitate capturing a small piece of the burgeoning online retail marketplace for our clients.

Our Promise

With CareClicks.com™, our partner organizations will achieve maximum revenue and supporter benefits, painlessly helping their respective communities attain the funds necessary to reach their goals.

Our Management Team

With over 50 years experience working with nonprofits, affinity groups, marketing firms, financial service organizations and internet technology, the executive management team at CareClicks.com™ brings a world of expertise to its client groups and supporters. They possess a thorough understanding of the needs and strengths of nonprofit affinity groups. Their passion for the significant opportunities that e-commerce affords these organizations, coupled with concern over current alternatives, inspired the development of the company.

Getting Started

There are 3 easy steps to begin saving money when shopping online, while also generating income for your group:

  • Always begin your online shopping at www.careclicks.com
  • Click on your organization from the group organization menu
  • Shop and Save

Yes, it's as simple as that. Do you want to make it even easier in the future? Bookmark (add to my favorites) the page where your organization name is listed at the top, and you eliminate steps one and two above. Once CareClicks.com™ is added to your favorites, right click to "send to" desktop (create shortcut). Your organization CareClicks.com™ page is now an icon on your desktop, "just a click away from showing you care"! It's as easy as that!

Shopper Benefits

  • Save Money - CareClicks.com™ provides shoppers with current coupon codes and promotions (free shipping, $20 off purchases of $50 or more, buy 1 get one free etc.)
  • Discover new options - In addition to your current favorite retailers, CareClicks.com™ will expand your shopping horizon to over 1,000 merchants.
  • Convenience - You are literally a click away from entering the CareClicks.com™ virtual mall. No more searching and typing to find the goods or service that you need.
  • Make your everyday purchases count - Every time you checkout at a CareClicks.com™ merchant, not only have you saved money, time, or both, but you create income for your group. CareClicks.com™ is truly a win-win for both you and your organization.
  • Privacy - No registration is required (name, address, telephone number, credit card information etc.) CareClicks.com™ respects your privacy, and wants to make your online shopping experience as simple and pleasant as possible. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for further details.

Organization Income

CareClicks.com™ is affiliated with over 1,000 quality online merchants, many of which are household names where you already shop. In return for our directing consumers to a merchant's website, they compensate CareClicks.com™ with a portion of each transaction, called an affiliate commission. Based on our unique "profit sharing" formula, we in turn forward 100% of standard base commissions and standard volume based performance bonuses (where applicable), less our administrative expenses and fees. The commission percentage range you see next to the merchant's name in our directory is exactly what your organization receives, with all expenses already reflected. Amounts vary from merchant to merchant, ranging anywhere from 1.0% for a $1,000 computer, to 30% for a $20.00 magazine subscription. Please refer to the Group Income section to the right of each merchant for exact compensation levels.

On average, the purchases you make will generate between 5.00-6.00% net revenue for your organization. In order to maximize earnings for our clients, we also pool all nonprofit partner sales activity to qualify for higher compensation levels, when applicable. This means more money raised for your group, even though alone, you would not have qualified. As you can see, CareClicks.com™ truly cares about its partnerships.
