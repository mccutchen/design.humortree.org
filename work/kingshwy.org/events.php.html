Events

The neighbors of Kings Highway are unique people. They represent many different ages, nationalities, professions and interests. Our regular gatherings and unique celebrations are a match for the spirit and diversity of the neighborhood. All events are open to Kings Highway residents and their guests. They provide a chance to connect with neighbors and learn more about the neighborhood. We are waiting for you.

Ongoing Events

  • First Fridays-Once a month, neighbors gather at a Kings Highway home for fun, news, and a chance to unwind from the week. For information about this month's location or to host a First Friday call 214-948-5936 or contact our Events Coordinator.
  • General Member Meetings-This once-a-quarter meeting is a chance to address issues specific to the Kings Highway Conservation District. Frequently guest speakers provide a forum for further discussion on local issues. Neighbors are given an intimate, open forum to discuss questions about a variety of topics, from code enforcement to safety concerns, from local to state government updates, and much more. Visit this website for date, location and subject.
  • KHCD Board Meetings-Elected and general officers of the Kings Highway Conservation District meet monthly to forward neighborhood goals through planning. This meeting is open to all and is held on the second Saturday of the month. Call 214-948-5936 or email our Events Coordinator for details.

Special Events

  • Annual Potluck and Silent Auction-Fundraising for neighborhood projects has never been more fun. This yearly party is great for socializing and supporting Kings Highway improvements. Late winter.
  • Neighborhood Garage Sale-Neighbors donate unwanted items to make this multifamily sale a huge draw. Located at Stone Park, this annual event generates funds for Kings Highway's operating budget and special projects. Spring.
