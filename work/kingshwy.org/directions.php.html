Directions

From the North

Take I-35 South to I-30 West. Exit Sylvan Avenue. Turn left and continue on Sylvan to Colorado Boulevard. Turn right on Colorado Boulevard and continue to the stop sign at Edgefield Avenue. Turn left on Edgefield Avenue. Continue to Stewart Street. The Kings Highway Conservation District is bordered on the North by Stewart Street.

From the South

Take I-35 North to Zang Boulevard. Exit Zang Boulevard. Head North on Zang Boulevard to 8th Street/Davis Street. Turn left on Davis Street. Continue on Davis Street to Tyler Street. The Kings Highway Conservation District is bordered on the South by Davis Street.

From the East

Take I-30 West to I-35 North. Exit Colorado Boulevard Turn right on Colorado Boulevard and continue past Methodist Hospital to Sylvan Avenue/Tyler Street. Turn left on Tyler Street and continue to Kings Highway. The Kings Highway Conservation District is bordered on the East by Tyler Street.

From the West

Take I-30 East to Hampton Road. Exit Hampton Road South and continue on Hampton Road past Fort Worth Avenue to Davis Street. Turn left on Davis Street. Continue on Davis Street to Mary Cliff Road. The Kings Highway Conservation District is bordered on the West by Mary Cliff Road.
