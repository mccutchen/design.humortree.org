Campaign Calendar

January 11, 2002 First day for early voting clerk to receive application for ballot by mail
February 11, 2002 Last day to register to vote in Primary
February 25, 2002 Early voting in person begins
March 2, 2002 Early voting for 12 hours on Saturday
March 3, 2002 Early voting for 5 hours on Sunday
March 4-8, 2002 Early voting for 12 hours, Monday through Friday
March 5, 2002 Last day to receive application for mail ballot
March 8, 2002 Last day to vote early in person
March 12, 2002 Primary election day. Polls open 7AM - 7PM
