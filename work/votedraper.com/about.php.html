About Charlene

* * *

Charlene Draper is married to Raplph H. Draper, a local minister and assistant superintendent with the Marshall Independent School District. They are the proud parents of three lovely daughters: Genesis, a senior at Spelman College, Atlanta, GA; Joy, a freshman at Lipscomb University, Nashville, TN; and Charity, a sophomore at Longview High School.

Some Quick Facts about Charlene

  • Member of New Hope Church of Christ
  • Assistant Youth Director
  • Bible School Teacher
  • Owner of D & D Affordable Homes
  • Graduate of the University of Texas in Communications
  • Elected to two terms on the Longview City Council for District 2
  • Serves on a number of community boards
