Longview Leadership Society
The Longview Leadership Society is an honorary organization that recognizes individuals who have made substantial personal monetary contributions to the Greater Longview United Way.

Any individual or couple who donates $1,000 or more to the Greater Longview United Way annual campaign qualifies for membership in the Longview Leadership Society.

  • Bronze -- $1,000 - $2,499
  • Silver -- $2,500 - $4,999
  • Gold -- $5,000 - $7,499
  • Platinum -- $7,500 - $9,999
Alexis de Tocqueville Society (recognized nationally) -- $10,000 and above
