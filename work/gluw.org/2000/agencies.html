United Way Agencies
The 21 member agencies of the Greater Longview United Way provide a wide range of human and social services. Each agency participates in an intensive annual programmatic and fiscal review conducted by the Admissions and Allocations committee of the Greater Longview United Way.

To find out more about any of these agencies, click on that agency's name in the list below.
