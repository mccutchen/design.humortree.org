Campaign 2002-2003

The annual fund-raising campaign fuels the activities of both the Greater Longview United Way and its member agencies throughout the coming year. The campaign is about much more than dollars; it is about folks caring for each other and about each other, and showing that by giving both money and time. Similarly, the United Way is about much more than just raising money; it is about "uniting the caring power of the community."

However, the dollars raised during the campaign make it possible for the Greater Longview United Way and its member agencies to achieve their ambitious goal of creating sustained improvement in the quality of life for all who live in the Greater Longview area.

The needs in Longview are greater than ever, and the generous spirit of its citizens is greater than ever. Because of these two facts, the campaign goal for 2002-2003 is more ambitious than ever:

$1,070,000

Who will make that happen? More than 400 volunteers, including Campaign Chair Rogers Pope Jr. and Campaign Co-Chairs Susie Paul and Ray Stroud Jr.

And there's a place for YOU! Contact the United Way office to volunteer. Together, we can show...

Our Way of Caring!
