Information and Referral

INFOline of Gregg County

  • (903) 236 9211

INFOline of Gregg County is a free community Information and Referral Service... a bridge between people who need assistance and the agencies that can address those needs.

A service of the Greater Longview United Way, INFOline of Gregg County can provide current information about:

  • Families and parenting
  • Adolescents and youth
  • Child care
  • Senior citizen services
  • Mental health
  • Substance abuse
  • Services for persons with disabilities
  • Housing
  • Transportation
  • Food assistance
  • Domestic violence
  • Medical/legal services
  • Support groups
  • Hotline numbers
  • And much more.

Hours: 9am to 5pm, Monday through Friday

Bilingual: INFOline has both English- and Spanish-speaking specialists on staff.

While INFOline of Gregg County provides information about current programs available in Gregg County, it does not endorse any agency or program. INFOline of Gregg County, a member of the Texas Alliance of Information and Referral Services and the national Alliance of Information and Referral Systems, received start-up funding from the Texas Department of Protective and Regulatory Services and from the Gregg County Health Care Fund.
