Information and Referral

INFOline of Gregg County

(903) 236 9211

INFOline of Gregg County is a free community Information and Referral Service... a bridge between people who need assistance and the agencies that can address those needs.

A service of the Greater Longview United Way, INFOline of Gregg County can provide current information about:

  • Families and parenting
  • Adolescents and youth
  • Child care
  • Senior citizen services
  • Mental health
  • Substance abuse
  • Services for persons with disabilities
  • Housing
  • Transportation
  • Food assistance
  • Domestic violence
  • Medical/legal services
  • Support groups
  • Hotline numbers

...and much more.

Hours:  9am to 5pm, Monday through Friday

Bilingual:  INFOline has both English- and Spanish-speaking specialists on staff.

INFOline of Gregg County is a member of the Texas Alliance of Information and Referral Services (TAIRS) and the national Association of Information and Referral Services (AIRS). While INFOline of Gregg County provides information about current programs available in Gregg County, it does not endorse any agency or program.
