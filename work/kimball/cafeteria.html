PAMS Lunchroom

PAMS (Parent Account Management System) Lunchroom is a secure, fast and friendly way for parents to pay online by credit card for their children's school meals. At PAMS Lunchroom, parents can set up automatic payment plans, view account balances and history, receive automated notification of balance status and more!
